LANGUAGE

Introduction

In order to bring a change in our society, we have focused ourselves on language training as well as computer science. our training program lasts 6 months, the course days are divided according to the programs.

English program

The course days for this program are divided according to the levels.

First level (beginners)

This level includes all learners or students who have just started the English training. Here, we introduce them to the language, we show them its advantages in different areas, they also learn basic notions, such as the English alphabets, vowels, consonantes and so on. They study every Monday, Tuesday and Wednesday from 6 p.m to 8 p.m.

Second level (average level)

This one includes those who already have an advanced level thanks to the notions acquired in the first level. In this level, we try to deepen the subjects so that our learners may finally be able to read, write and express themselves. Some chapters or points are succeptibles in this level, such as the parts of speech, etc.. The course hours are the same as those of the first level.

Third level (terminal level)

In this one, new learning techniques arise. The learners, in having a very advanced level (hight level) gotten thanks to the conceptes acquired in the second level, become able to speak fluently, to understand differnet accents of people, to talk to anyone, to lead an activity. They can also present exposé, grammar points and so one.

Indeed, this level offers our learners the opportunity to practice what they have learned in the lower grades (inferior classes). These activities are held every Thursday at the same time.

Each level lasts two months. The students form the second level are strongly encouraged to take part in the third level activities in order to gether themselves and to improve more. After spending 6 months of learning, our learners are certified through a celebration held every july the 2nd or 3rd. However, there are some standards to respect in order to be certified in our center.
Check them here.

French program

Actually this program is not complete yet, only the third level is in operation. The activities take place every Friday at the same time.
