ABOUT

Let talk about the evolution of the center

By the stronger desire to achieve his goals and the dedication to charing his knowledge, Mr Héritier as the visionner decided to found an English center.

He started teaching English at his own way in 2016 at different places, such as schools, families and so on. At the first tuition which was held at "Lycée cibitoke" located in Burundi / Bujumbura, he had twelve students, and the training took end after realising that his students were able to speak fluently English. However it was not the end, he was admired and sollicited by so many people, then he kept on teaching at the same place.At that time the club was called E.T.C (English Training Center).

In 2019, he got associated to an open association in Bujumbura whose's mission is to fight against poverty ALDP (Association de Lutte contre la Délincance et la Pauvreté), this association is led by Mr.ANACLE KITUNGANO. This one made the center one of his branches the 6th june 2019, then imediately the center changed the name from E.T.C to F.F.B.F (Fighting For a Better Future).

Missions and visions

  1. To initiate the youth for the international languages, such as French and English additionally Informatique science/ technology.

  2. Promote the development of the community (country) by being bilingualistic.

  3. Eliminate all distractions not use for youth in order to follow serious.

  4. To add the capacity of language and technology that was lent by them to different ereas.

  5. To empower or to help the youth to reach their goals with all needed things.

  6. To empower youth so that they may be their own bosses (Entrepreneurship).

  1. Become a popular English center and get impact in the society

  2. Teach both English and French without forgetting technology or computer science

  3. To create extension in so many places here in Burundi even outside the country for the same purpose

  4. Be teaching English online to easy students to learn no matter where or who they are

  5. Working hand by hand with ALDP association in order to accompany it to a durable change

The representative's history

Although he was born in Tanzania he is congolese, his father's name is MCHUMBE ISHIBALEKA and his mother's name is JOHARI ISSA MTITI, after finishing his primary school in Tanzania, they shifted with his family to DRC where he did a short part of his secondary school before coming in Burundi where he finished and got his diploma certificate in 2021.

Meanwhile he was studing he was learning english in participating in defferents clubs and in making reseaches, he was very couragous and visionary, he has faith that one day he would know english and train it to others, finally by his courage and his perseverance he made it and amazingly english became his bread winning activity, he innovated a club where he has been teaching since 2017 up to now, he has taught many people and now they are good at speaking english.

Really it was not a piece of cake to achieve his goals, beause he failed many times, but in spite of that he did not give up, because of english he was about to fail in 3rd form secondary school with 52% wherease he was always at the tree first places.
